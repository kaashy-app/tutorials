How to Delete Your Kaashy Account

If you've decided to delete your Kaashy account, follow the steps below carefully. Remember, this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Step-by-Step Guide

1 Open the Kaashy App

Launch the app on your phone and make sure you're logged in.

2 Go to Settings

Tap on the Settings icon from the main navigation bar or menu.

3 Tap "Delete Account"

Select "Delete Account" from the list of settings options.

4 Review Important Information

Before proceeding, Kaashy will show you important points:

  • Deleting your account is permanent.
  • Withdraw any balance from your wallet before continuing.
  • Your transaction history and data will be permanently deleted unless required by law.

5 Confirm Deletion

Tap the red "Delete Account" button at the bottom of the screen.

6 Final Confirmation

A confirmation message may pop up asking you to confirm. Accept to complete the deletion process.

Tip:

Make sure your wallet balance is zero before attempting to delete the account, otherwise the process won't continue.
